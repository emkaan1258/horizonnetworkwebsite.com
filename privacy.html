Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Horizon Network LLC ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use horizonnetworkplans.com or contact us for a consultation.

1. Information we collect

We collect only what we need to help you:

  • Contact details you provide — such as your name, phone number, and email address.
  • Consultation details — such as your ZIP code or address, current provider, usage, and budget, which you share so we can advise you.
  • Basic technical data — such as general analytics about how the site is used (for example, pages visited), which may be collected through standard web tools.

2. How we use your information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and provide the advisory consultation you requested;
  • Process your $10 consultation fee;
  • Communicate with you about your request;
  • Improve our website and service.

3. We do not sell your information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not share your details with internet or cable providers unless you explicitly ask us to on your behalf.

4. Service providers

We may use trusted third-party tools to operate our business — for example, an email form provider to receive your messages or a payment processor to handle the consultation fee. These providers only access the information needed to perform their function and are expected to keep it secure.

5. Payment information

Any payment for the $10 consultation is handled by a third-party payment processor. We do not store your full card details on our servers.

6. Cookies & analytics

Our site may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality and anonymous analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some features may not work as well if you do.

7. Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide our service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. You may request that we delete your information by contacting us.

8. Your choices

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to stop contacting you, by emailing support@horizonnetworkplans.com .

9. Children's privacy

Our service is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
