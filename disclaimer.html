Summary: Horizon Network LLC sells independent advice only. We are not an internet or cable provider, we are not affiliated with any provider, and we cannot guarantee any specific plan, price, deal, or saving.

1. Independent advisory service

Horizon Network LLC ("Horizon Network," "we," "us," or "our") operates horizonnetworkplans.com as an independent consumer advisory service. For a flat $10 consultation fee, we help consumers understand and compare internet and cable plans and choose an option that fits their needs. We provide guidance and information only.

2. We are not an internet or cable provider

We do not sell, resell, supply, activate, install, repair, or service internet, cable, television, or phone service. We are not a telecommunications carrier and do not provide technical support for any product or service. Any service you decide to obtain is purchased by you directly from the provider of your choice, under that provider's own terms and pricing.

Their use does not imply any relationship between Horizon Network LLC and those companies.

Our only charge is a flat $10 advisory fee, disclosed to you before any payment is taken. We do not receive commissions, referral fees, or any other compensation from internet or cable providers. We have no financial incentive to steer you toward any particular provider.

Plans, speeds, prices, promotions, and availability are set entirely by the providers and change frequently and without notice. Purchasing a consultation does not create any ongoing obligation on our part to manage your service, monitor pricing changes, or act on your behalf with any provider. Our role ends when the consultation is complete, unless separately agreed in writing.
