Common questions

Good to know before you call

Are you an internet or cable company?
No. Horizon Network LLC is an independent advisory service. We are not a provider and are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or authorized by any internet or cable company. We help you compare and choose — you sign up with the provider directly.
What exactly does the $10 fee cover?
It covers a consultation in which we review the plans available at your address, compare them neutrally, and give you a clear recommendation plus the fine print to watch for. The fee is disclosed before you pay and is our only charge.
Do you get paid by the providers?
No. We do not receive commissions or referral payments from any provider. Our only income is the flat advisory fee you pay us. That's what keeps our advice neutral.
Can you guarantee the lowest price or a specific deal?
No. Plans, prices, and promotions are set by the providers and change frequently. We help you understand and compare your options, but we can't guarantee savings, availability, or any specific outcome.
Do you set up or install the service?
No. We provide advice only. After your consultation, you contact and contract with the provider yourself to start, change, or install service.
